Who we are

A student led consulting group built around clarity, discipline, and real partner value.

Horizon brings together students interested in strategy, engineering, product, operations, analytics, and entrepreneurship. Members learn by doing real consulting work with real stakeholders.

We focus on professional development through live projects, structured training, mentorship, and a high standard for final deliverables.

Our process

From vague question to clean recommendation.

Discover

Define the real problem

We align on the partner need, key constraints, stakeholders, and project scope.

Analyze

Build the evidence base

We gather data, run research, interview users, and identify patterns that matter.

Recommend

Create a clear direction

We translate insight into options, tradeoffs, and a recommendation partners can act on.

Deliver

Leave momentum behind

We close with polished deliverables, implementation steps, and a confident partner handoff.
