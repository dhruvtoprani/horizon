Who we are

A student-led consulting group built around real projects, technical support, and professional growth.

Horizon exists to give MSU students earlier access to relevant hands-on experience while helping early-stage companies and growing organizations across Michigan access technical support.

Members work across disciplines, communicate with external organizations, and build technical, analytical, consulting, leadership, and project management skills through real-world projects.

Our process

Every project starts with scope, expectations, and responsible execution.

Scope

Define the objective

We identify the partner need, client contact, team members, timeline, expected deliverables, and project limits.

Build

Assemble the team

Project teams are selected based on commitment, interest, professionalism, willingness to learn, and fit for project needs.

Execute

Manage the work

Teams complete assigned work, attend meetings, communicate professionally, and protect confidential project information.

Deliver

Hand off responsibly

Partners receive student-led analysis and recommendations while retaining responsibility for review and implementation.
